SUMMARY

My name is Seth, or "Seth A". The "A" isn't an actual initial of mine, but the intial of an old alias I used to go by (and "Seth A." sounded better than "Seth G." or "Seth M." in my opinion).

I've been making music since ~2015, although I've only started to become fairly consistent with making new tracks at the start of 2019. I aim to make at least one track a month, hopefully more than that.

The music I make normally consists of a combination of synths and orchestral/acoustic instruments like pianos, guitars, and strings. In the future I want to obtain more orchestral VSTs/plugins in order to make purely orchestral & acoustic pieces, moving away from synths.
