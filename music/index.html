SETH GIBBS

MUSIC

My name is Seth Gibbs, I've been making music since ~2016, although I've only started to become fairly consistent with making new tracks at the start of 2019. I aim to make at least one track a month, hopefully more than that.

The music I make normally consists of a combination of synths and orchestral/acoustic instruments like pianos, guitars, and strings. In the future I want to obtain more orchestral VSTs/plugins in order to make purely orchestral & acoustic pieces, moving away from synths (but not discarding them completely).
