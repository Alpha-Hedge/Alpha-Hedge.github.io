I've been making music since ~2016, although I've only started to become fairly consistent with making new tracks at the start of 2019. I aim to release at least one original song per month (not including remixes and the like).

The music I make normally consists of a combination of synths and orchestral/acoustic instruments like pianos, guitars, and strings. I don't have a particular genre I usually aim for, it tends to just be whatever I feel like making at the time.
